About Us

KMCT ITM refers to KMCT Institute of Technology & Management in Kuttippuram, Malappuram, Kerala, which is an educational institution offering various programs in engineering, technology, and management. It provides B.Tech, BBA, BCA, MCA, MBA, and diploma programs, and is affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University. The institution focuses on developing technological expertise and a culture of innovation through a quality education system
